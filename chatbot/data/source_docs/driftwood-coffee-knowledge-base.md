# Driftwood Coffee Roasters — Customer Help Center

*Last updated: March 2026. This document is the official source for customer support answers.*

---

## About Driftwood

Driftwood Coffee Roasters is a small-batch coffee subscription company founded in 2019 and based in Portland, Oregon. We roast to order and ship freshly roasted beans directly to subscribers across the United States and Canada. We are not currently able to ship outside the US and Canada.

Our customer support hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM Pacific Time. We are closed on weekends and US federal holidays. You can reach us at support@driftwoodcoffee.example or through the chat widget on our website.

---

## Subscriptions

We offer three subscription tiers:

- **The Daily** — 1 bag (12 oz) per delivery. $16 per delivery.
- **The Household** — 2 bags (12 oz each) per delivery. $30 per delivery.
- **The Office** — 5 bags (12 oz each) per delivery. $70 per delivery.

You choose your delivery frequency at checkout: every 1, 2, or 4 weeks. Beans are roasted the day before they ship, so they always arrive fresh.

You can pause, skip, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard under "Manage Subscription." Pausing keeps your settings and roast preferences saved. There is no cancellation fee and no minimum commitment.

If you skip a delivery, you are not charged for that cycle. Changes must be made at least 48 hours before your next scheduled roast date, or they will apply to the following delivery instead.

## Roasts & products

We offer four house roasts year-round:

- **Harbor Light** — light roast, bright and citrusy. Single-origin Ethiopian.
- **Morning Tide** — medium roast, balanced and smooth. Our most popular option.
- **Deep Current** — dark roast, bold and chocolatey.
- **Still Water** — decaf, medium roast, naturally water-processed.

All beans are sold whole. We can grind your order at no extra charge — select your grind type (whole bean, drip, espresso, or French press) in your subscription preferences. We recommend whole bean for the freshest cup.

Seasonal limited-edition roasts are released four times a year and are available to subscribers one week before the general public.

## Shipping & delivery

Orders ship via ground carrier and typically arrive within 3 to 5 business days of the roast date. Shipping is free on all subscription orders. One-time (non-subscription) orders under $25 have a flat $5 shipping fee.

We currently ship to all 50 US states and all Canadian provinces. Canadian orders may take an additional 2 to 4 business days to clear customs.

You will receive a tracking link by email once your order ships. If your package is marked delivered but you can't find it, please wait 24 hours (carriers sometimes mark early) before contacting support.

---

## Returns & freshness guarantee

Because coffee is a perishable product, we cannot accept returns of opened bags. However, we stand behind our freshness.

If your coffee arrives damaged, stale, or you're simply not happy with it, contact us within 14 days of delivery and we'll send a free replacement bag or issue a full refund — your choice. You do not need to ship the original bag back.

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

---

## Billing

Subscriptions are charged automatically on each roast date. Your card is charged when the order is roasted, not when you originally signed up.

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. We do not currently accept Apple Pay or cryptocurrency.

If a payment fails, we retry once after 3 days. If it fails again, the subscription is paused until you update your payment details.

To update your payment method, go to your account dashboard under "Billing."

---

## Account & login

You can update your shipping address, roast preferences, delivery frequency, and payment details anytime from your account dashboard at driftwoodcoffee.example/account.

If you forgot your password, use the "Reset Password" link on the login page. Reset links expire after 1 hour.

Address changes must be saved at least 48 hours before your next roast date to apply to that delivery.

## Wholesale & gifting

We offer gift subscriptions in 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month prepaid options. Gift recipients can set their own roast preferences. Gift subscriptions do not auto-renew.

For café, restaurant, or bulk wholesale inquiries, email wholesale@driftwoodcoffee.example. Wholesale pricing and terms are handled separately from the consumer subscription tiers above.
